BIO


Mariya Holbrook is a graphic designer and freelancer. She is an accomplished creative thinker, with a keen eye for detail, a strong technical ability and a boundless enthusiasm for love, life, design, illustration, photography and a tasty riff.


With a particular interest in flyer and identity design, Mariya enjoys immersing herself in any given design brief, working closely with clients on projects both above and below the line.


Mariya is currently hunting for challenging new freelance, part-time and full-time graphic design positions, so please contact her if you would like to discuss a new design brief, or recruitment for an exciting new role in your studio.
